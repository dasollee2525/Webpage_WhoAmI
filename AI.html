The Artificial Intelligent Projects that I've done

Semi-Supervised Learning for Detecting Korean Malicious Comments

2021.09~2021.12

It is obvious that there is the limitations of labeling all malicious comments manually.
To solve the problem, the research is design on NLP and semi-supervised learning.
We developed a BiLSTM4VAT artificial intelligence model using Pytorch to improve BiLSTM to enable Virtual Adversarial Training (VAT).

A Study on AI-based Info-vaccine Production Method to Prevent Infodemic in Social Media

2020.12~2021.09

By simulating the actual process of producing and distributing vaccines to fight viruses,
we study how to design and produce "info-vaccines" that can respond to infodemic on social media.
The research was based on NLP(Natural Language Processing) and conducted using Twitter data and BERT model.
